FAQ

Frequently Asked
Questions

Everything you need to know about our web design process and services.

Q How fast can you deliver my website?

Free one-page sites are delivered in 3-5 days. Basic sites take 7 days, Growth sites 10-14 days, and Pro sites 14-21 days. We'll give you a specific timeline when you start.

Q What does "unlimited revisions for 30 days" mean?

For 30 days after delivery, we'll make unlimited small changes like text edits, color adjustments, and layout tweaks. This doesn't include complete redesigns or adding major new features.

Q What's included in the "free" one-page site?

Everything listed in the One-Page Starter package: professional design, mobile responsive, contact form, social links, and unlimited revisions for 30 days. You only pay if you need hosting.

Q What content do I need to provide?

You'll need to provide: your business name, logo (if you have one), business description, services/products, contact information, and any photos you'd like to use. We can help source stock images if needed.

Q Do you provide hosting and maintenance?

Yes! We offer hosting and maintenance for $29/month, which includes updates, backups, security checks, and minor content edits (up to 15 minutes per month).
